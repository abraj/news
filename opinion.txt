Wikipedia vs Concise.org:
    * Exhaustive vs Concise
    * Informative vs Introductory
    * Bureaucracy vs Democracy
    * Review after publish vs Review before publish
    * Non-reviewed/peer-reviewed vs peer-reviewed/(subject-matter expert)
    * Voluntary vs Remuneratory
    * Research vs Basic Education
    * Encyclopedia vs Open-source-course-books-repository/(schools,colleges)

concise.org:
# /news/..
    * Daily News
    * On-going ``temporary`` issues
# /notes/.. [unique]
    * Suggestion: Redlinks (validate need, classify typeof)
    * Articles/Nouns {Linkable} [Typeof: Person, Place, Thing] : Steve Jobs, India, Vector, Speed, Velocity, ...
    * Articles/Info  {Linkable} [Typeof: Event, Issues, Info, Questions]: Israel-Palestine Issue, ...
    * Articles/Tutorials {Linkable} []: Newton's first law
# /notes/.. [collection/merge/explain/comprehensive-flow/]
    * Pack articles together with a few explanations in-between so that the flow makes sense
    * Topics {Linkable} []: Newton's laws of motion
# /modules/..
    * Chapters : Depends on class and capability ...
    * Module
# /modules/examples/..
    * solved examples, numericals
* Course
* Subject
